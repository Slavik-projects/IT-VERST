Our Approach

01.

Large-scale distributed system

We take up-to-date technologies, best practices, and big market players' expertise as a basis for designing, launching, operation, and development of various kinds of apps, concerning social networks and content delivery systems, e-science and machine learning solutions, with the performance and scalability maintaining.

02.

Microservices architectures

Our team has passed through all stages of web development evolution and has started to use all the benefits of microservice architecture pattern, which provides the capability to make real-time production deployments. This greatly reduces the need for the outdated monthly or weekly big production deployments.
